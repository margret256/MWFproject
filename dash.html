OUR COMPANY

Mayondo Wood and Furniture is a growing local workshop dedicated to crafting high-quality, custom-made wooden furniture. Located in Uganda, the company is committed to delivering durable, elegant, and
affordable products to meet the everyday needs of customers — from homes to offices.

OUR EMPLOYEES

At the heart of the company is a hardworking and passionate team of employees, including skilled carpenters, painters, designers, and delivery staff.
Each employee plays a critical role in the daily operations — from selecting materials to finishing products and ensuring safe delivery.
The team works closely under the guidance of the manager to fulfill customer orders on time and with excellent quality.
